Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try Netpin before committing?

Absolutely! Our Starter plan is free forever, and Pro comes with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required.

What counts as a "cluster"?

A cluster is any Kubernetes cluster connected to Netpin, regardless of size. Whether it has 1 node or 1,000, it counts as one cluster.

Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?

Yes! You can change your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be prorated for the remainder of the billing cycle.

Is my data secure?

Security is our top priority. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We're SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant.

Do you offer discounts for startups?

Yes! We have special pricing for early-stage startups. Contact our sales team to learn more about our startup program.

What happens if I exceed my cluster limit?

We'll notify you before you hit the limit. You can either upgrade your plan or disconnect some clusters to stay within your quota.
